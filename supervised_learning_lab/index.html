What is Linear Regression?

Linear regression finds the best-fit straight line through data points to predict continuous values.

When to Use It

Used for predicting numerical outcomes like prices, temperatures, or scores.

Key Concepts

  • Slope (m): Rate of change
  • Intercept (b): Y-value when X=0
  • R²: How well the line fits (0-1)
  • Correlation: Strength of relationship (-1 to 1)
