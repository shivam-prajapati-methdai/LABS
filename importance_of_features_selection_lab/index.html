🎯 What are Features?

Features are the clues we give the computer to help it make a good guess.

For our house, the features are the things we can change with the sliders. The more clues we give, the better the guess!

🏠
House Size

Square footage is a feature

🛏️
Number of Bedrooms

Room count is a feature

🚿
Number of Bathrooms

Bathroom count is a feature

📅
House Age

Age in years is a feature

🌿
Lot Size

Land area is a feature

Key Point: Features are the "inputs" - the information we know about something that helps us make a prediction.

🎪 What is the Target?

Target is what we want to predict or estimate.

In our house example, the target is the house price - that's what we're trying to figure out!

Key Point: The target is the "output" - what we're trying to predict using our features.

🧠 What is a Model?

Model is like a smart calculator that learned from many examples to understand patterns.

Our model learned from thousands of house sales to understand how features affect price!
